The Case for CricketX Pro

Problem → Solution

The Problem

Scoring cricket is still stuck in the past

  • Paper scorebooks get wet, lost, and can't be shared after the match
  • Manual run rate and RRR calculations create scorer errors and slow the game
  • No digital record — player stats and match history vanish after every match
  • Club organizers can't track team performance or player growth across the season
The Solution

CricketX Pro — one tap scores the game

  • Cloud-saved scorecard after every ball — zero risk of ever losing a record
  • Auto run rate, RRR, strike rate, economy — completely zero manual math
  • Shareable scorecards as images and PDF — WhatsApp-ready in one tap
  • Full match history, player profiles, and AI-powered team analytics
